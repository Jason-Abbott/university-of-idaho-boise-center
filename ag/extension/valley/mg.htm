Horticulture and the Master Gardening Program

Direct your horticulture questions to us! The Master Gardening Program is very strong in Valley County, and we have Master Gardeners who specialize in anything from cold-climate gardening to composting. They will help you grow a productive and beautiful garden.

If you are interested in signing up to become a Master Gardener, contact the Extension Office. The next series of classes will be held in the winter of 1999.

Other services

  • The Master Gardeners volunteer to do gardening projects in gardens open to the public and provide information/education for classes of all ages. Call the Extension Office to schedule programs or gardening volunteer work.
  • The Extension Office has a library with video tapes, pamphlets and books on gardening. These can be checked out.
