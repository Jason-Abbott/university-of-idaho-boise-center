4-H in Valley County

Vision

4-H... A leader in developing Idaho's youth to become responsible, productive citizens, meeting the changing needs of a diverse society.

Mission

4-H... Helping Idaho youth acquire knowledge, life skills, and attitudes that enhance their lives.

How to register

  • Any youth between 5 and 19 years can enroll.
  • Enroll anytime between Fair and June 30.
  • Enroll at the University of Idaho Extension Office in Cascade.

Costs

  • $10 for horse
  • $8 for general projects
  • $4.50 for cloverbud
These cover 2 projects - additional projects are $1.00 each.
