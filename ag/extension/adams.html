Gordon C. Keetch, Associate Extension Professor and County Chair
M.S. and B.S., Utah State University

Appointment: 100% Extension (90% Extension Education and 10% Administration). Extension: Extension Educator - Natural Resources and Livestock. Keetch develops educational programs to effect optimal livestock production, natural resource management, youth development and rural revitalization. His program emphasis is in beef production and includes cow-calf management, feedlot performance, carcass merit and marketing. He currently is conducting a district-wide educational project in innovative beef marketing, "A to Z Retained Ownership Company". He also trains volunteer leaders to develop youth through the 4-H program. Administration: Keetch is County Chair of the Adams County Extension Office. The responsibilities of this appointment include office personnel management, liaison with the county commissioners, and coordination of county programs.
