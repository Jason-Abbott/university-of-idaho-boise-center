Judging Team Results

Seven Gem/Boise County 4-Hers recently attended the University of Idaho Ag Days. Brian Hill, Angie Obermeyer, Kaila Spencer, EmmyLou Swander, Rachel Swanson, Elizabeth Taggart Nieman and Dusty Warren participated in the livestock and dairy judging contests as teams and as individuals. In the livestock judging contest, first and eighth place individual scores were earned by Rachel Swanson and Elizabeth Nieman. Gem/Boise teams placed third and seventh. In the dairy judging contest Gem/Boise teams place first and third. In addition they toured the Ag Center, attended a University of Idaho football game and a pre-game barbeque. Thanks to Beth Kienitz and Laurie Christison for taking our teens up to Moscow for this event.
