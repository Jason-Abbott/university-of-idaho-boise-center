Mir-M Seyedbagheri, Extension Professor and County Chair
M.S. and B.S., Utah State University

Appointment: 100% Extension (95% Extension Education and 5% Administration). Extension: Extension Educator - Crops. Seyedbagheri identifies and defines problem areas then plans, implements, and evaluates field demonstrations. His applied research is based on field and laboratory data, as well as field research results developed by universities, private businesses, and government agencies. His work focuses on six crops in Elmore, Owyhee, and eastern Ada counties--potatoes, sugar beets, small grains, alfalfa, mint, beans, as well as pasture management. Seyedbagheri teaches and disseminates information on farm production efficiency and profitability, adoption of Integrated Pest Management (IPM) and other sustainable practices. Seyednagheri educates the public on maintaining and improving soil and water resources in urban horticulture and gardening. He teaches and administers educational youth projects, emphasizing 4-H leader training, youth development, and school enrichment. Administration: County Chair of the Elmore County Extension Office and Educational Director of the Weed and Pest Departments. The responsibilities of this appointment include office personnel management, liaison with the county commissioners, and coordination of county programs.
