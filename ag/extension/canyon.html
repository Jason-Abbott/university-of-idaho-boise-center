Darrell G. Bolz, Extension Professor and County Chair
M.S. and B.S., University of Idaho

Appointment: 100% Extension (90% Extension Education and 10% Administration) Extension: Extension Educator - Crops. Bolz major program emphasis is in crop production practices, integrated pest management, irrigation, and farm management for Canyon, northern Owyhee, and western Ada counties. A major component of the crops program is focused on seed crops in Canyon county. He serves as liaison with the Idaho Alfalfa Seed Association and the Northwest Alfalfa Seed Association. He also conducts an educational program in urban horticulture that includes a Master Gardener program, media releases, and clientele contacts. Administration: Bolz serves as County Chair of the Canyon County Extension Office. The responsibilities of this appointment Include office personnel management, liaison with the county commissioners, and coordination of county programs.

Kathleen Cheldelin, Associate Extension Professor
M.Ed., University of Idaho; B.S., California State University

Appointment: 100% Extension. Extension: Extension Educator - 4-H/Youth Development. Chedelin plans and implements extension educational programs and activities for optimal development of youth and families. She coordinates the 4-H horse program for Canyon and Ada counties. Emphasis on youth development, life skills, leadership, conflict management, volunteer education and school age child care.

Beverly Montgomery, Extension Professor
M. Ed., Albertson College of Idaho; B.S., University of Idaho

Appointment: 100% Extension. Extension: Extension Educator - Family and Youth Development. Montgomery assesses needs, plans and implements educational programs in family development, nutrition, diet and health; food safety and preservation. In 4-H and youth development, Montgomery's primary focus is home economics, science, arts, and personal development. Her major 4-H responsibilities are leader and member recruitment and training.

Katherine Roy, Assistant Extension Professor
Ph. D., Washington State University; M.S. Oklahoma State University; B.S. Washington State University

Appointment: 100% Extension. Extension: Extension Educator - Livestock. Roy plans and implements extension educational programs in dairy management, beef production, and forage improvement for producers in Canyon and Ada counties. She also conducts educational programs for sheep and swine producers in District II and serves as a resource for 4-H livestock programs. For the past three years, she and Darrell Bolz have developed and conducted workshops for small-acreage landowners in Canyon and Ada counties.
