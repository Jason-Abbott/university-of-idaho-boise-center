Extension Nutrition Program

What is the Extension Nutrition Program (ENP)?
  • A free local nutrition education program
  • The program is taught by Nutrition Advisors trained by the University of Idaho Cooperative Extension System

Who is it for?

  • Low income families with children

What will I learn?

  • Be a better shopper
  • Keep food safe
  • Make quick meals
  • Prepare easy, low cost meals
  • Feed your children well
  • Make money last longer
Where will we meet?
  • In your home
  • A local church
  • A community center
  • The Extension office
  • With a group of friends
  • One on one
When I finish?
  • When you complete the lessons you will receive a certificate from the University of Idaho Cooperative Extension System
How do I get started?
  1. Contact the ENP office at 376-1036, 5880 Glenwood, Boise, ID 83714, or
  2. Send an e-mail to efnepii@uidaho.edu, or
  3. Complete the ENP form on the next page and mail it to the ENP office
