Jan 20
12:30-5:30 p.m.		 Practical Food Safety for Food Service Workers - 5880 Glenwood Street, Boise. Learn important sanitation, food preparation, food cooling and storage concepts at this 4 ½ hour training. Participants will complete requirements for Idaho's mandatory food safety certification. Cost is $10 if received by January 15, $15 afterwards. Registration required. Fill out the information below. For more information call Rosa Smith at the Payette County Extension System - 642-6022.
Feb 10 & 24
9:00 a.m.-1:00 p.m.		 Advanced Master Food Preserver Training - Ada County Extension office, 5880 Glenwood St., Boise. If you have completed the first year MFP class you are eligible to become an Advanced Master Food Preserver. Requirements are 3 advanced classes and 20 hours of volunteer work. It is fun and you learn up-to-date food preservation techniques. Call Vivian at 377-2107 if you would like to participate.
Feb 10
10:00 a.m.		 Retirement Planning and Pitfalls - Meridian Library, 1326 W Cherry Lane. No Charge. Your thoughts on retirement may include happy anticipation, plans of how to meet your financial goals, and concern that you are facing all the investment issues you should. Cyndi Kay of Prudential Securities will help you better understand the financial and investment choices you should be making as you prepare for retirement. Some questions that will be covered: How do I plan the retirement I want? Will I have enough retirement income? Are my savings invested wisely to meet my retirement goals? Is there other investment tools I can use to prepare for retirement? What should I do today to build my retirement plan? Does my plan protect me if I hit a financial bump along the way? Must pre-register - call 377-2107.
