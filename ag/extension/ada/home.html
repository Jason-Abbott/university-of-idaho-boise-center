The Ada County Extension Office is a county land grant office of the College of Agriculture at the University of Idaho. We provide programming in 4-H youth development, family and consumer sciences and horticulture. We offer classes such as the Master Gardener, Master Food Preserver, food safety, personal finance and more!

Available at the Ada County Extension Office, Resources for Idaho (Bulletin 401). This new catalog lists hundreds of University of Idaho Extension publications. You can also learn about Extension publications on the Internet: http://info.ag.uidaho.edu
