    Information:
    Winter Gardening Tip
    • Cottony masses of mealybugs may begin to show up on indoor plants. A cotton swab dipped in rubbing alcohol can sufficiently remove them or use an insecticide.
    • Stake and tie evergreens and other shrubs to prevent heavy snows from breaking branches.
    • Keep holiday plants well watered and in as much light as possible. Avoid exposing the plants to drafts and overheating.
    • With poinsettias, after leaves have dropped, cut stems just below last colored bracts. Place plant in cool, dark place with sparse water until May 1.
    Gardening tip from Treasure Valley Gardening Guide
