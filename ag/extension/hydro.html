Timothy D. (Tim) Stieber, Assistant Extension Professor
M.S. and B.S., University of Wisconsin-Madison

Appointments: 75% extension, 10% research, 15% administration. Extension: Extension Educator - Water Quality. Stieber develops and delivers educational programs and technical assistance to help agricultural producers meet state water quality goals. He provides nutrient, pesticide, and water use recommendations for the project and establishes on-farm demonstrations of best management practices (BMPs). Stiever facilitates technology transfer between federal and state agencies, soil conservation districts, and landowners. Research: He develops and evaluates nutrient, irrigation, and pesticide BMPs and documents resource and economic benefits of BMPs applied by producers. Administration: Stieber administers water quality project funds and coordinates activities with all other agencies involved with water quality in southwestern Idaho.
